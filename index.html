All the help for your Digital Technologies journey

Design and Technologies can sometimes be an overwhelming subject. There are many skills and knowledge items that you need to be all over. This website will help you navigate the digital waters of zeroes and ones and brings you straight to the correct tutorials to help you get the most for your projects.

Each year group is separated in various sections:

Computer Science

This section will cover the main topics in class, explain various terms and definitions and help you with questions to check your understanding.

Design Theory

A website is nothing without some good looks. Design theory will teach you the key terms per year groupd and have examples and simple projects to practise.

Skills

In the Skills labs, you will find appropriate tutorials and practice projects for you to follow along to help you nail your school projects. It will cover the Adobe Suite, Scratch and coding depending on your year group

Projects

In the Projects section, you will find all the projects that you will be working on your skills and knowledge. This is THE place to combine your knowledge and skills and find your own gaps.
